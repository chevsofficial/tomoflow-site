Section 1 – Getting Started

1. What is TomoFlow?
TomoFlow is a focus and productivity app based on the Pomodoro Technique. It helps you break work into focused sessions with short breaks, manage tasks, and see how much real focus time you’re getting through simple analytics.
2. What is the Pomodoro Technique?
The Pomodoro Technique is a time-management method where you work in focused blocks (traditionally 25 minutes), followed by short breaks (usually 5 minutes). After several cycles, you take a longer break. This structure helps improve focus, reduce mental fatigue, and avoid burnout.
3. Who is TomoFlow for?
TomoFlow is designed for students, remote workers, freelancers, and anyone who wants to focus better on deep work, studying, side projects, or daily tasks without getting overwhelmed.
4. Do I need an account to use TomoFlow?
No. You can fully use TomoFlow offline without creating an account. Creating an account is optional and mainly used for cloud sync across devices.

Section 2 – Timers & Sessions

5. How do Pomodoro sessions work in TomoFlow?
You pick (or create) a task, start a focus session, work until the timer ends, and then take a break. TomoFlow logs:
  • The planned duration of the session (e.g., 25 minutes)
  • The actual time you spent (including pauses / resumes)
  • Whether the session was completed, skipped, or cancelled early
These logs feed into your analytics.
6. Can I change the default work and break durations?
Yes — you can customize your focus and break durations in Settings, but this is available only for TomoFlow Pro users. Free users use the default Pomodoro-style durations.
7. What happens if I pause the timer or finish early?
If you pause and resume a session, TomoFlow still measures the elapsed time in the background, but your analytics track the planned focus time (to avoid inflated numbers caused by pauses).

If you tap End & Save before the timer finishes, TomoFlow records the exact focus time you completed (for example, ending at minute 10 of a 25-minute session logs 10 minutes).
8. Why did a session show as “Cancelled early (not counted)”?
If you skip or cancel a work interval within the first 5 minutes, TomoFlow may label it as “Cancelled early (not counted)” and exclude it from your analytics so accidental starts don’t affect your stats.
9. Why doesn’t a skipped session show in my analytics?
Very short, skipped sessions (e.g., cancelled in the first few minutes) are intentionally not counted as real focus time. This keeps your focus charts clean and only shows sessions where you actually worked for a meaningful stretch.
10. Do I need to keep the app open for the timer to work?
No, but you should allow notifications. TomoFlow uses system notifications to remind you when a session or break ends, even if the app is in the background. If notifications aren’t working, check:
  • System notification permissions for TomoFlow
  • “Do Not Disturb” / Focus modes on your device

Section 3 – Tasks, Activity Types & Limits

11. How do I create and manage tasks?
You can add tasks in the Tasks screen. Each task can be:
  • Created with a title (and optional details)
  • Edited after creation
  • Marked as Done
  • Deleted if you no longer need it (we recommend exporting your data before deleting tasks if you want to keep a record)
You can then start focus sessions directly from a task.
12. Is there a limit to how many tasks I can create?
Yes. Free users can create up to 10 tasks.
TomoFlow Pro includes unlimited tasks.
13. What are Activity Types in TomoFlow?
Activity Types let you categorize your work beyond just the task name. For example:
  • Deep Work
  • Admin / Email
  • Study
  • Creative
  • Meetings
This allows your analytics to show how much time you spend on each type of work, not just per individual task.
14. Is there a limit to how many Activity Types I can create?
Yes. Free users can create up to 3 Activity Types.
TomoFlow Pro includes unlimited Activity Types.
15. What happens if I run a session with no task or no Activity Type selected?
If you start a work interval without selecting a task, or the task has no Activity Type set, TomoFlow automatically groups that time under a default Activity Type like “Other” in your analytics. This ensures all sessions are tracked and nothing gets lost.
16. Can I change a task’s Activity Type later?
Yes. You can edit a task and change its Activity Type. Future sessions will use the updated Activity Type. Past analytics stay associated with whatever Activity Type was active at the time of the session.

Section 4 – Analytics & Progress

17. What’s the difference between Planned Time and Actual Time?
Planned Time: the intended duration of the session (e.g., a 25-minute focus block).
Actual Time: elapsed time from start to finish including pauses (for example, a 25-minute plan could take 30 minutes if paused/resumed).

Important: TomoFlow analytics primarily track Planned Time to measure real intended focus and avoid inflated numbers caused by pauses. However, if you use End & Save, analytics will record the actual completed focus time (e.g., ending at minute 10 logs 10 minutes).
18. How does TomoFlow calculate my focus time?
Your focus time is based on completed work intervals where you actively ran the timer. Very short cancelled sessions are excluded. If you tap End & Save, TomoFlow counts the completed focus time up to that point (instead of the full planned session).
19. Why does my Actual Time look higher than my planned session?
If you pause and resume over multiple segments, the elapsed time can exceed the planned duration. This reflects real-world interruptions.

Note: TomoFlow analytics do not use this “Actual Time” for focus totals. Focus totals are based on planned time (or End & Save completed time).
20. Why is a session missing from my analytics?
Possible reasons:
  • It was skipped very early and marked as not counted
  • It was a break-only interval (some views only show work intervals)
  • The app didn’t fully save the session due to being force-closed or low memory
If it keeps happening, you can contact support so we can investigate.
21. Can I see which Activity Types I spend the most time on?
Yes. In the analytics screen you’ll see breakdowns by:
  • Total focus time
  • Task and/or Activity Type
  • Date range (today, week, month, etc., depending on the version)
This helps you understand where your real focus is going.

Section 5 – Accounts, Sync & Data

22. What syncs across devices?
If you create an account and enable cloud sync, TomoFlow can sync:
  • Tasks
  • Activity Types
  • Session history (focus logs)
  • Most key settings (depending on version)
This allows you to move between devices without losing your data.
23. Can I use TomoFlow offline?
Yes. Core features like timers, tasks, and local analytics work offline. Sync-related features will update once you’re back online.
24. How do I delete my account or data?
Until an in-app delete option is added, you can email:
  • support@tomoflow.app
  • Subject: “Delete My Data”
We’ll process your request and delete your account and associated cloud data within a reasonable time, as described in the Privacy Policy.

Section 6 – Free vs Pro (TomoFlow Pro)

25. What’s included in the free version?
The free version includes:
  • Pomodoro-style timer
  • Task creation and management (up to 10 tasks)
  • Activity Types (up to 3)
  • Core analytics (limited date range)
  • No ads (TomoFlow does not include ads)
26. What does TomoFlow Pro unlock?
TomoFlow Pro unlocks:
  • Unlimited tasks
  • Unlimited Activity Types
  • Cloud sync across devices
  • Export data to CSV
  • Premium themes
  • Advanced analytics with unlimited date range
27. Is there a free trial for TomoFlow Pro?
Yes. The annual TomoFlow Pro plan includes a 7-day free trial (subject to App Store / Play Store rules). If you don’t want to continue, just cancel before the trial ends to avoid being charged.
28. How is billing handled?
All billing is handled by:
  • Apple App Store on iOS
  • Google Play Store on Android
You can manage or cancel your subscription in your store account settings. We don’t see or store your credit card details; subscription status is checked via a secure service (like RevenueCat).
29. Do I have to upgrade to Pro to use the app?
No. The free version is fully usable as a focus timer with basic tasks and analytics. Pro is for users who want:
  • Unlimited tasks and Activity Types
  • Cloud sync
  • CSV export
  • Premium themes
  • Analytics with an unlimited date range

Section 7 – Notifications & Sound

30. How do I enable or disable notifications?
On first launch, TomoFlow may ask for notification permission. If you denied it, you can re-enable notifications in your device settings under Notifications → TomoFlow. From inside the app, you can also adjust:
  • Sound on/off for session end
  • Vibration (if supported)
  • Volume or notification type (platform dependent)
31. The timer ends but I don’t get any alert. What should I do?
Check:
  • Device system notifications for TomoFlow are allowed
  • Your phone is not in Do Not Disturb / Focus mode that hides alerts
  • The app’s own notification/sound settings are enabled
If everything looks correct and it still doesn’t work, please contact support.

Section 8 – Troubleshooting & Misc

32. The timer stops or behaves strangely when I switch apps. Why?
Some devices are aggressive about battery saving and may limit background timers. Try:
  • Disabling battery optimization for TomoFlow (if your OS allows it)
  • Keeping the app in the recent apps list instead of force-closing it
  • Ensuring you’re on the latest app version
33. My stats don’t look right. How can I fix them?
Double-check:
  • Whether very short sessions are excluded
  • If you changed time zones recently
  • If you edited tasks or Activity Types that were used heavily
If you still see something that looks off, take screenshots and email us at support@tomoflow.app so we can review and improve.
34. I got a new phone. How do I bring my data over?
If you used TomoFlow with an account and cloud sync, just:
  • Install TomoFlow on your new device
  • Log in with the same account
Your synced tasks and history should appear. If you used TomoFlow without an account, your data stays on the old device only.
