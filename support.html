Support

Need help with TomoFlow? We’re here for you.

Contact us

The fastest way to reach us is by email: support@tomoflow.app

When you contact us, it helps if you include:

  • Your platform (iOS, Android, or Web)
  • Your app version (from settings, if possible)
  • A short description of the issue
  • Screenshots or screen recordings, if relevant

Common questions

Do I need an account to use TomoFlow?

No. You can use TomoFlow entirely offline and without an account. Accounts are only needed if you want optional cloud sync across devices.

How do I delete my data or account?

If you have created an account and want to delete your data, please contact us at support@tomoflow.app. In future versions, we plan to add a self-service option directly in the app.

Does TomoFlow work without internet?

Yes, core timer and tasks work offline. Some features, like cloud sync or future web app features, may require an internet connection.

Feedback and feature requests

We’re actively improving TomoFlow and love hearing how you use it. If you have ideas or feature requests, email us with the subject line “Feedback” so we can find it easily.
